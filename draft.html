What We Verify

Five areas of verification

Here's what they cover.

01

Personnel Controls

People are the most common point of failure in data security. This section covers the policies and practices at the human layer.

What we look for: background checks are conducted on personnel with access to sensitive information, a password policy or enforcement settings, automatic screen lock on workstations, a process for revoking access when personnel leave or change roles, and controls for remote system access if applicable.

02

Email Security

Email is often where merchant documents first arrive and where they're most vulnerable.

What we look for: MFA or 2FA enabled, and at least one safeguard for attachments that pass through email — e.g. automated CRM routing, Aquamark's email watermarking tool (or equivelant provider), forwarding or downloading restrictions, etc.

03

CRM / Portal Security

Your CRM or deal management platform houses sensitive merchant data and documents.

What we look for: MFA or 2FA enabled, role-based access so personnel only see what their role requires, and at least one control on document access — such as view-only settings (documents not downloadable), watermarking, DLP policies, etc.

04

Document Storage

Applicable if documents are also stored in cloud storage, e.g. Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive

What we look for: sharing or download restrictions, watermarking, etcw.

05

Outsourcing

Applicable when using third-party teams for deal processing..

What we look for: download restrictions, watermarking documents, or other controls that limit external distribution.

Process

How certification works

Step 1

Apply

Submit request to apply and get listed in the directory. Requirements don't need to be met at the time of application — you'll have 90 days to complete verification.

Step 2

Verify

You will receive access to the self-guided portal where you can submit payment for the membership fee, and upload your evidence. Each section explains what's needed and provides example pathways. Message our team directly inside the portal if you have questions about any requirement.

Step 3

Review

We review your evidence, run business background check, and provide a Results Summary Report indicating the status of each requirement. If everything checks out, your certification is issued. If further information is required, it'll be noted on your report.
