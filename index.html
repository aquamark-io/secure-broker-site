Join the brokers making responsible data handling visible through independent verification.

Apply Requirements Directory

Our mission is to help establish a standard for how merchant data is protected in alternative finance. The program gives the industry a way to identify responsible operators through evidence-based, independent verification. The goal is simple: help brokers and funders make better partnership decisions and reduce the data misuse that harms small business owners and the companies that serve them.

ABOUT

With nearly 20 years in alternative finance, Christina brings deep operational experience spanning ISO operations, funder relationships, and the day-to-day realities of how merchant data moves through the industry. She founded Aquamark to address industry-wide data security challenges she witnessed firsthand, and created Secure Funder and Secure Broker to establish a practical framework for self-governance.
