Our Mission

Secure Broker™ is a new industry initiative helping lead alternative finance toward stronger standards around trust and accountability. In a market with limited barriers and inconsistent oversight, data misuse continues to harm business owners and undermine brokers who choose to operate with care. Paired with Secure Funder™, the programs independently verify companies taking practical steps to protect merchant data and move the industry forward.
