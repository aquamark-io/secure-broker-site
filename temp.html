About

Why Secure Broker Exists

Brokers are the first link in the chain. When a merchant submits their bank statements, tax returns, or financial documents, those files land in a broker's hands before anyone else sees them.

Yet there's no standard for how brokers store, share, or protect that data. Some brokerages have strong internal controls. Many don't — and merchants have no way to tell the difference.

Secure Broker was created to close that gap. It gives brokers a way to demonstrate to merchants, funders, and partners that they handle sensitive data responsibly.

The program is independent, the requirements are public, and verification is conducted by a third party — not the broker themselves.

The industry is growing.
Its standards should grow with it.
